OVERVIEW
This interactive demo recreates the mesmerizing background effects and card animations from Balatro using Three.js and custom GLSL shaders. Click anywhere to play the music and move your mouse to experience parallax effects.

Three.js
GLSL Shaders
WebGL

Particle Systems

• Custom fluid shader background
• Audio-reactive animations

• Particle effects on card corners
• Mouse-controlled camera parallax
• Pixel-perfect card design
